ECDD: Advance the laser by one frame
Used by the routines at E6F6 and E76C.
Input
HL Current screen position of laser
DE Current laser bolt graphic
A Colour to draw the laser with
Output

ECDE PUSH DE
ECDF LD (HL),A
ECE0 LD A,H
ECE1 AND $03
ECE3 RLCA
ECE4 RLCA
ECE5 RLCA
ECE6 OR $40
ECE8 LD H,A
ECE9 LD B,$08
ECEB LD A,(DE)
ECEC LD (HL),A
ECED INC H
ECEE INC DE
ECEF DJNZ $ECEB
ECF1 POP DE
ECF2 POP HL
ECF3 RET
